# Story on Steroids — Product

## Product Purpose
Landing page for a professional video production service for Instagram Stories. The service shoots professional-grade story content for businesses and entrepreneurs who currently use only their phone.

## Users
Israeli business owners, entrepreneurs, and marketers aged 25–45 who post Instagram Stories to promote their business. They're aware their phone-shot content looks amateur but don't know how to upgrade. They respond to social proof, results, and sharp contrasts between before/after.

## Brand Voice
Bold. Direct. Premium but approachable. Israeli-market confident. The brand name "Story on Steroids" says it all — power, transformation, results. Not luxury-quiet, not shouting — commanding.

## Register
Brand (landing page — design IS the product)

## Color Strategy
Committed — deep black base (#000) with electric cyan (#00d4ff) as the hero accent. Secondary: warm red for "before" states. The cyan carries 30–40% of interactive surface area.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (bland gradients, Inter font everywhere)
- Cheap video production ads (loud yellow/red clashing)
- Corporate photography sites (cold, sterile white)

## Strategic Principle
The before/after comparison section is the most important conversion element on the page. It must be immediately interactive and show an unmistakable quality gap in the first 3 seconds.
